# The Learning Enhancement Team

The learning enhancement team (LET) is part of library and learning enhancement (LLE) and is based in the library building at UEA.  The team offer support across the University for:

* Academic writing
* Mathematics
* Peer Assisted Learning (PAL)
* SpLD
* Statistics
* Study skills

The service offers:

* In-course workshops
* Weekly drop-in sessions (Mathematics and Statistics helpdesk, Study cafe, Study together)
* Reading and writing retreats for post-graduates
* One-to-one 50 minute tutorials, in-person and online
